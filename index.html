In our First Annual, Hogs for Wiener Dogs event, Diamond Dachshund Rescue, will bring both motorcyclist and drivers together to enjoy a leisurely drive through the Hill Country to benefit the homeless dogs of our community cared for by Diamond Dachshund. All proceeds will be used to provide much needed medical care for these abandoned and abused Dachshunds. Our loving fosters will provide them with shelter, food, and love. Registration can be completed online or in person on the day of the event.
Itinerary
  • 9:00am Registration
  • 10:00am Wheels Up
  • 11:00am Pit Stop 1
  • 12:00pm Pit Stop 2
  • 1:00pm Hill Country Distillery
There will be great food, awesome raffles, sale items, and a chance to ride along the beautiful scenic hill country. All of this to help support the dachshunds of Diamond Dachshund Rescue.
